About

MindConnect connects you anonymously with a psychologically trained person for a private, confidential conversation. No registration. No recordings. Each session lasts 45 minutes.

Our approach is research-driven. We believe access to psychological support should not depend on income or geography. This platform documents and enables that mission.

01

Connect

Click "Connect" and the system automatically pairs you with an available counsellor, usually within two minutes.

02

Talk

You have 45 minutes of video or text conversation. You lead, talk about whatever you need.

03

Stay anonymous

No account required. No session recordings. The conversation is entirely private and disappears when you leave.

This platform is not a substitute for professional psychiatric care. In a crisis, please contact a mental health helpline or emergency services.

The project

MindConnect is an independent project with a single goal: make psychological support accessible to everyone, free of charge, anonymously, without waiting weeks for an appointment.

Privacy

No registration. No conversation storage. Sessions run peer-to-peer wherever technically possible, meaning no data passes through a central server.

Counsellors

Volunteers with psychology education: students, graduates, or certificate holders. Each has completed a brief screening and introductory training.

Project developed by Andrej Milković as an independent research and humanitarian initiative. No commercial interests.
